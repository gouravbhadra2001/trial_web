<==Seeking ingenious intellects


Looking to ignite the spark of creativity in your writing? Look no further than our writers' club! Our community of wordsmiths is passionate about the craft of writing and eager to share our experiences and insights with one another. Whether you're an aspiring novelist, a seasoned poet, or simply looking to flex your creative muscles, our club offers a welcoming and supportive environment for all. And with our brand new website, designed with care by one of our talented student members, it's never been easier to connect with like-minded writers and stay up-to-date on all our latest events and activities. So come join us and let's put pen to paper together!

<==Our Founder


Mr. Das, a passionate writer and literature lover, struggled for years to establish this writers' club in his locality. Despite facing numerous obstacles, his determination and love for writing kept him going. As he looked upon the club's establishment, he said, "Writing is the breath of my soul, and this club is my home for the soul." Mr. Das holds a master's degree in English literature and a bachelor's degree in creative writing.
